Employee Device Onboarding Guide

1. Welcome

This guide helps new employees set up their company‑issued devices.

2. What You Receive

-   Laptop
-   Charger
-   Optional monitor and accessories
-   Quick start instructions

3. First‑Time Setup

1.  Power on and connect to WiFi.
2.  Log in using your company email.
3.  Complete security setup (MFA, password, device encryption).

4. Support

For issues, contact IT through the service desk.

5. Responsibilities

Employees must maintain the device, report issues promptly, and follow
security protocols.
