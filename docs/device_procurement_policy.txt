Device Procurement Policy

1. Purpose

This policy defines the process for sourcing, approving, and delivering
devices to employees across global locations.

2. Scope

Applies to all company–issued laptops, monitors, peripherals, and mobile
devices.

3. Procurement Process

3.1 Device Requests

Employees submit requests through the internal IT portal, specifying
role requirements and justification.

3.2 Approval Workflow

IT validates technical requirements. Finance reviews and approves
budget.

3.3 Ordering

Approved devices are procured through approved vendors. All devices are
asset‑tagged and registered in the Rayda system.

3.4 Delivery

Devices are shipped using Rayda’s logistics partners and tracked until
successful delivery.

4. Documentation

Invoices, warranties, and ownership documents must be uploaded to
Rayda’s Fixed Asset Document Manager.

5. Risk Management

Lost or damaged devices must be reported within 24 hours. Devices
reaching EOL are wiped and recycled responsibly.
